Foul

Description

Foul is considered the most famous dish among Egyptians. Nearly All Egyptians from all social stratas eat and love this Egyptian dish.

Ingredients

  • Fava Beans
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Leave the fava beans in water for a day
  2. take the beans from the water and boil them for 12 hours
  3. take the beans and put on them vegetable oil and salt

other ingredients can be used such as tomatoe sauce and peppers, and foul is usually ate with lemons and onions.
