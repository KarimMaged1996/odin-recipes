Shawerma

Description

Shawerma is considered an Egyptian delicacy. It is served in thousands every day. It is a sanwitch and is usually eaten in the street. shawerma can be served as chicken or beef.

Ingredients

  • olive Oil
  • Freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • onion
  • garlic cloves
  • Chicken breast, thighs or beef
  • spices such as salt, cumin powder, turmeric, sweet paprika, & black pepper

Steps

  1. Chicken or beef is marinated in the fresh lemon juice and the spices
  2. it is then grilled or broiled
  3. served in bread after that
